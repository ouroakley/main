Vaccinations are available **from 13 April to 30 June** for:

- adults aged **75 and over**
- residents in **care homes for older people**
- **immunosuppressed** patients

**This clinic:** Saturday **25 April** at Overton Surgery. **Limited capacity** — book early if you are eligible.

You can also **call [119](tel:119)** between **7 April and 29 June** to book appointments at community pharmacies across the area.
